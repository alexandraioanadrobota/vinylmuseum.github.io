Visit the Vinyl Museum

We are open Tuesday to Sunday, from 10am to 6pm. We are closed on Mondays. Admission prices are as follows:

  • Adult (ages 18+): $15
  • Senior (ages 65+): $10
  • Student (with valid ID): $10
  • Child (ages 6-17): $5
  • Children under 6: Free

When you arrive at the museum, please proceed to the ticket counter where you can purchase your tickets. We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards for ticket purchases.
